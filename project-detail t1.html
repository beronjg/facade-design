The Challenge

The project involved challenges related to mixed vertical and horizontal terracotta tile orientations, substructure customization, and material tolerances. Precise fabrication drawings with orientation-specific tolerances and mock-up validation ensured accurate alignment and visual consistency, while iterative detailing of brackets and clips accommodated project-specific loads and curvatures without affecting ventilation or aesthetics. Systematic tag marking linked to drawings minimized sequencing errors for large tile quantities, and batch-specific fabrication adjustments were implemented to manage natural terracotta variations and maintain uniform joint widths.

Problem: Coordinating complex terracotta tile orientations, customized substructure fixings, and material tolerances while ensuring accurate sequencing and consistent façade aesthetics at scale.
